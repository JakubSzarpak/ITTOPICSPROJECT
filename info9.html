Social Media Security: Protecting Your Digital Identity

Explore best practices for securing your social media accounts. Learn about privacy settings, the importance of limiting personal information exposure, and how to recognize and avoid social engineering attacks.

Social Engineering Definition:

Social engineering is a manipulation technique used by cybercriminals to trick individuals into divulging confidential information or performing actions that compromise security. Maintaining privacy settings and being cautious about sharing personal information helps protect against social engineering attacks on social media.
