Data Backup Basics: Protecting Your Digital Assets

Discover the importance of regular data backups to prevent data loss. Explore different backup methods, including cloud storage and external hard drives, to ensure the safety of your important files.

Data Backup Definition:

Data backup involves creating copies of important files or information to prevent data loss in the event of hardware failure, accidental deletion, or other unforeseen incidents. Backup methods include cloud storage, external drives, and automatic backup solutions.
