Understanding Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Enhance your account security by implementing two-factor authentication. Learn how to enable 2FA on various platforms and understand the additional layer of protection it adds to your online accounts.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Definition:

2FA is a security process that requires users to provide two different authentication factors before gaining access to an account or system. Typically, this involves something the user knows (password) and something the user has (security code from a mobile app or text message).
