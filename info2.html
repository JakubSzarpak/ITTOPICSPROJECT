Introduction to Cybersecurity: Protecting Yourself Online

Learn the basics of cybersecurity to safeguard your digital life. Understand common threats such as phishing and malware, and explore preventive measures like installing reputable antivirus software and keeping your system updated.

Cybersecurity Definition:

Cybersecurity involves the practice of protecting systems, networks, and digital data from theft, damage, or unauthorized access. It includes measures to prevent, detect, and respond to various cyber threats, such as malware, phishing, and hacking.
