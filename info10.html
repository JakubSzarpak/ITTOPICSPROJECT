Networking Essentials for Beginners

Familiarize yourself with networking basics, including IP addresses, routers, and protocols. Understand the fundamentals of local area networks (LANs) and the internet, and explore resources for furthering your knowledge in networking.

Networking Definition:

Networking involves the practice of connecting computers and other devices to share resources and information. IP addresses, routers, and protocols are fundamental components. Local Area Networks (LANs) connect devices within a limited geographical area, while the internet is a global network of networks. Further exploration of networking concepts is essential for a deeper understanding of how data is transmitted and communicated across different devices.
