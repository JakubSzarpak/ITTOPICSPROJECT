Mastering Email Etiquette and Security

Efficiently manage your emails while maintaining security. Learn about phishing scams, how to recognize suspicious emails, and the importance of not clicking on unknown links or downloading attachments from unfamiliar sources.

Phishing Definition:

Phishing is a cyber attack method where attackers use deceptive emails or messages to trick individuals into revealing sensitive information, such as usernames, passwords, or financial details. Recognizing and avoiding phishing attempts is crucial for email security.
